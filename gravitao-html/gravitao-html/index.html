What is ConGreen

ConGreen is a concrete infused with moss spores.
ConGreen tackles air pollution and inefficient energy use in buildings by:

Regulating Temperatures

Moss retains moisture, which helps regulate air temperature by naturally cooling the surrounding environment. This cooling effect reduces the need for artificial climate control, making buildings more energy efficient.

Purifying Air

Moss absorbs airborne pollutants through its surface, filtering out harmful particles and improving air quality. Its natural ability to capture toxins makes it an effective and eco-friendly solution for cleaning the air.

Green Aesthetics

ConGreen not only improves functionality but also adds a natural, lush appearance to buildings, blending eco-friendly benefits with attractive design.

Why Moss?

Moss is nature’s own air purifier and temperature regulator, thriving without roots while offering a delicate yet powerful touch to concrete.

Its ability to absorb pollutants and moisture makes it a natural ally in our quest for cleaner air and greener buildings. Plus, its vibrant, textured surface adds a unique, verdant charm to any structure, marrying functionality with beauty.

How it Works

Step 1

We mix recycled cement with moss spores to get a self-sustaining moss that can be attached to any vertical surface.

Step 2

Using some bio gel made by naturally occurring fibres to hold the moss together.

Step 3

It requires basically no maintenance and can absorb and deal with the harshest of conditions.

Step 4

It absorbs CO2 and keeps the air clean while regulating airflow, reducing your air conditioning and heating bills.
